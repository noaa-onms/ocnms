Education and outreach represent major program investments for the sanctuary that help to engage community members of all ages and backgrounds in sanctuary activities and conservation efforts. OCNMS staff offer a variety of ways for people to deepen their understanding of the sanctuary, including through classroom and field-based lessons for students, speaker series events for general audiences, and ‘telepresence’ activities that share deep sea research and exploration efforts with audiences worldwide.
