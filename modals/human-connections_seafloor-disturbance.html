Certain human activities such as coastal construction, mooring installation, sediment sampling, some fisheries, and research activities are likely to disturb seafloor communities and potentially injure sanctuary resources and qualities. Permitting of some human activities that have the potential to do harm is one way the sanctuary tries to minimize disturbance of the seafloor. However, the sanctuary does not manage fisheries.
