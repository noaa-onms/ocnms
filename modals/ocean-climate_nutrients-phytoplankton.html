Maps - Columbia River plume

Hickey et al. 2013

Conceptual framework of environmental conditions for harmful algal blooms (HABs) in the OCNMS (from Hickey et al 2013). Idealized transport of toxic Pseudo-nitzschia cells from semi-retentive offshore waters in the U.S. Pacific Northwest under three types of environmental conditions. Surface currents are shown with arrows. The notations “Barrier” and “Conduit” refer to the role of the Columbia plume in preventing HABs from reaching coastal beaches, or accelerating HAB transport along the coast, respectively. Razor clam beaches are shown in red.
